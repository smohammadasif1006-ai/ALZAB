About Us:

Established in 1989, Alzab Algharbiyah Company has grown into a trusted name. With decades of experience, the company delivers quality, reliability, and value across multiple sectors.

Core expertise includes construction, infrastructure development, project management, and oil & gas sectors. Supported by skilled professionals and strong supplier partnerships, Alzab consistently meets high safety and quality standards.

Alzab believes in long-term partnerships built on integrity, transparency, and innovation.
