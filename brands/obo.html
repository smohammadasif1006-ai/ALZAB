About the Brand

World-Class Cable Management

OBO Bettermann is a world-leading manufacturer of installation systems for the construction and infrastructure sectors. Products include cable trays, conduits, floor boxes, earthing systems, and lightning protection components.

Superior Engineering Segments

TBS – Transient & Lightning Protection

Advanced earthing and surge protection systems to safeguard buildings and electronics from electrical strikes.

UFS – Underfloor Systems

Premium floor-integrated power and data distribution, perfect for open-plan offices and modern workspaces.

VBS – Connection & Fastening Systems

High-quality solutions for cable connection and fastening, ensuring secure and durable electrical installations.

KTS – Cable Support Systems

Versatile cable trays, ladders, and support structures for professional cable management in all architectural environments.

BSS – Fire Protection Systems

Certified insulation and cable routing systems designed to maintain integrity during fire emergencies.

Intelligent trunking and distribution paths for organized and accessible wiring across walls and ceilings.

Integrated power and data sockets, switches, and device connection sets for residential and commercial rooms.
